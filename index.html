Introduction

I am now a postdoctoral scholar at Purdue University, working with Prof. Dongyan Xu. I obtained my PhD from Fudan University, China, in summar 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Min Yang. Part of my work was also supervised by Prof. Guofei Gu from Texas A&M University, and Prof. Xiaofeng Wang from Indiana University at Bloomington.

My research focuses on data-driven mobile security and privacy. I use program analysis techniques, machine learning, and natural language processing to solve practical security and privacy problems on mobile platforms. Topics that I am currently working on are included below:

  • Privacy leakage detection on smart devices
  • Efficient program analysis for security enhancement
  • Software security compliance check
